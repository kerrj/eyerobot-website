We develop a mechanical eyeball with 2 degrees of freedom and a fish-eye lens that can be co-trained with a robot arm through BC-RL loop.

Real-World Task Execution Results

We evaluate EyeRobot on 5 tasks involving manipulation over a 210° workspace to probe the limits of hand-eye coordination over a large region.
